About

Meet Melissa, a talented web, UX, and social media designer. With a keen eye for detail and a love of creating beautiful and functional designs, Melissa is always looking for new ways to use her skills to make a positive impact on the world.

Whether she's designing a website for a local business, creating a user-friendly interface for a new app, or developing a social media campaign to promote a worthy cause, Melissa always brings her creativity and passion to the table.

When she's not working, Melissa can be found spending time with her horse, Beau, hiking in the mountains with her dog, Fox, or practicing at the archery range.
